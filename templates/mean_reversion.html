Mean Reversion Strategy

This is a strategy that first all constituents of the S&P 500 are tested for stationarity. The Means of the stocks that are stationary are then calculated. The main strategy here is to buy stocks whose current price is less than the mean, with the hope that stock prices tend to revert towards the mean
